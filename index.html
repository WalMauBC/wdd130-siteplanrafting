Overview

Purpose

As the owner of Dry Oar, I would like to build the revenue of my white water rafting tour companies. Currently, customers have to find our information through social media and have to call into our office to make an appointment. The purpose of this website is to increase revenu by making it easier for customers to book their tours online and give us an engaging website we can use as a landing page for marketing efforts.

Audience

The primary audience for this website is leaders of youth adventure groups. This could include scout troups or church groups looking for high adventure. They are males 30-55 years old. They have stable income and have jobs that allow them to take time off to mentor youth. They are leaders who love the outdoors. They are looking for activities to give their youth adventurous experiences in nature.
